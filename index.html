About Corona Virus

The coronavirus, also known as COVID-19, is a highly contagious respiratory illness caused by a virus called SARS-CoV-2. It was first identified in Wuhan, China in late 2019 and quickly spread to become a global pandemic.

About More

Coronavirus Symptoms

Coronavirus include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing, and the virus is primarily spread through respiratory droplets from infected individuals.

How to Protect Yourself

"Wear a mask, wash your hands frequently, and practice social distancing."

Wear Mask

Wearing a mask is one of the most effective ways to prevent the spread of the COVID. It is primarily spread through respiratory droplets that are released when an infected person talks, coughs.

Read More

Wash Your Hands

It is important to wash your hands frequently,before and after eating, after using the restroom, after coughing or sneezing, after touching high-touch surfaces, and after being in public places.

Read More

Stay at Home

One of the most effective ways to prevent the spread of COVID-19 is to stay at home and avoid unnecessary social contact. This is important for those who are at higher risk , such as older adults and childrens.

Read More

Keep social distancing

To reduce the risk of transmission, Maintain a distance of at least six feet from others, especially those who are not from your household. This is because people without symptoms can still spread the virus.
